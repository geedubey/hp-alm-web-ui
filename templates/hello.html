Hi, team!

This app was inspired by the need to quickly view and edit defects in our internal HP ALM instance.

Defects editing is quite limited. ALM workflow doesn't work. I.e. you should manually follow the workflow. I.e. when you change the status to fixed, don't forget to assign QA person

It is meant to be highly tailored to our needs.

It is largely experimental and needs some dirty workarounds to work (like the cross origin extension you've installed).
